## Admission Information

### Eligibility Criteria
1. Passed 12th Grade (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
2. Minimum aggregate score of 60% in qualifying examination
3. Valid JEE (Main) or KEAM score
4. Age limit: Generally not exceeding 25 years
5. Must be an Indian national or NRI/PIO/OCI as per government regulations

### Required Documents
- 10th and 12th mark sheets (original and attested)
- JEE (Main) or KEAM scorecard
- Aadhaar card
- PAN card
- Birth certificate
- School leaving certificate
- Community certificate (if applicable)
- Medical fitness certificate
- Migration certificate (if applicable)
- Passport-size photographs (4-6 copies)
- Transfer certificate

### Fee Structure (Approximate)
- **Tuition Fee per Semester**: ₹70,000
- **Other Charges per Year**: ₹15,000
- **Hostel Charges per Year**: ₹80,000
- **Total Approximate per Year**: ₹155,000

### Admission Process
1. **Application** (January - July)
   - Fill online application with personal details

2. **Merit Evaluation** (Ongoing)
   - Based on JEE Main/KEAM score and academic records

3. **Counseling** (July - August)
   - KEAM counseling or college-specific interviews

4. **Admission** (August - September)
   - Complete admission formalities and fee payment
